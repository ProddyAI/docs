Proddy is an all-in-one workspace management platform that helps teams collaborate, plan, and execute work efficiently from a single place.

It brings together communication, task management, and project tracking so teams can stay aligned without switching between multiple tools.

## What you can do with Proddy

With Proddy, you can:

- Organize work using Boards, Tasks, and Calendars
- Collaborate with your team through Chats and Notes
- Manage multiple workspaces and channels
- Track progress and stay updated in real time

## How Proddy is structured

Proddy is organized around a few core concepts:

- **Workspaces** – Separate environments for teams or projects
- **Channels** – Topic-based spaces inside a workspace
- **Boards** – Visual tools to plan and track work
- **Chats** – Real-time conversations with your team
- **Tasks & Calendar** – Tools to manage deadlines and schedules

Understanding these concepts will help you navigate Proddy easily.
