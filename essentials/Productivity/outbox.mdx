## **What youâ€™ll see in Outbox**

The Outbox displays:

- Messages sent in channels
- Note exports (HTML / PDF / JSON / Markdown)
- Canvas shares
- Calendar-triggered messages
- System-generated activity entries

Each entry shows:

- Source channel (e.g., #general)
- Content preview
- Timestamp (date & time)

## **Access the Outbox**

1. Open the **Proddy workspace**.
2. Click **Outbox** from the left sidebar.
3. The Outbox opens showing all sent messages and activities.
