### **How to access it?**

\*\*Manage \*\*is available in the sidebar of your workspace.

1. Expand the sidebar.
2. Click on \*\*Manage \*\*to open the \*\*Manage \*\*page.

# **Workspace**

### **Renaming a Workspace**

To rename a workspace:

1. Open the \*\*Manage \*\*page
2. Under the \*\*Workspace \*\*tab, find the **Workspace Name** option inside **Workspace Settings.**
3. Type in the new name and click **Save**.
4. Your workspace name will be updated.

# **Channels**

This tab manages all the active channels inside the workspace.

To access the \*\*Channels \*\*tab:

1. Open the **Manage** page.
2. Select \*\*Channels \*\*from the given tab menu.

image placeholder


### **Creating a new Channel**

To create a new channel:

- Open the \*\*Manage \*\*page.
- Go to the \*\*Channels \*\*tab as directed above.
- Click on \*\*the New Channel \*\*option.

Image placeholder

- Enter a name for the channel, and select an emoji (optional)

image placeholder

Then click on **Create Channel, **to add it to your channels list.


### **Edit a Channel**

To edit a channel in the workspace:

1. Open the **Manage** page
2. Go to the **Channels **tab as directed above.
3. Click on the edit icon  next to the active channels.
4. After editing, click on **Update Channel** to save your edits


### **Deleting a Channel**

To delete a channel in the workspace:

1. Open the **Manage** page
2. Go to the **Channels **tab as directed above.
3. Click on the delete icon  next to the active channels.
4. Confirm your choice by clicking on **'Delete'**.

\
**Caution**: This action cannot be undone. This will permanently delete the channel and all of its messages.



# **Members**

Manage workspace members and permissions. This section allows administrators to invite new users, assign roles, and control account access levels.

To access it,

1. Open the **Manage **page.
2. Select the **Members tab** from the given tab menu.

image placeholder


### **Inviting new Members**

You can invite new members to your workspace by sharing your workspace link and code with them. To know more about joining a workspace, <u>click here</u>.

To invite a member:

1. Open **the Manage** page.
2. Go to the Members tab as directed above.
3. Click on **Invite People**.
4. Copy the link and code and share it with your teammates.

Image placeholder


### **Changing the workspace code**

To create a new code for the workspace.

1. Open **the Manage** page.
2. Go to the Members tab as directed above.
3. Click on the refresh icon  to change the workspace code.
4. Share it with your teammates to help them join your workspace


### **Editing User Permissions**

To change user permissions, 

1. Open **the Manage **page.
2. Got to **the Members** tab as directed above.
3. Click on the user icon next to the user name.
4. Select from the following options
   1. Make Owner
   2. Make Admin
   3. Make Member

image placeholder 


### **Removing a User**

To remove a user from the workspace,

1. Open **Manage**  page
2. Go to the Members tab as directed above.
3. Click on the Delete icon  next to the users to remove their access to the workspace.
4. Click on **Remove Member** to confirm your action.

**Caution**: This action cannot be undone. This will remove the member from your workspace and delete all of their messages and reactions.



# **Integrations**

This tab shows settings related to connecting your workspace with various other third-party apps. This effectively increases productivity of the workspace by integrating all into one place.

To access it:

1. Open the **Manage** page.
2. Select **Integrations **from the given tab menu.

image placeholder

You can integrate any of the following third-party services: 

- GitHub
- Gmail
- Slack
- Jira
- Notion
- ClickUp

\
**Note**: You can refresh each connection by clicking on the **Refresh **button.
