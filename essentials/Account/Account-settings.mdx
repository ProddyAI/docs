### Overview

In Account Settings, you can set up and adjust these settings:

- Your name
- Account Details
- Privacy Settings
- Delete account

### **Profile**

To update your default personal avatar with a custom one:

1. In the upper-right corner, click your personal avatar.
2. Select **Account Settings**.

3. In the **Profile** section, click your default avatar. 
4. Click the camera icon  to upload an image.

### **Privacy Settings**

This setting controls your privacy and status visibility. By disabling it, you choose to not display when you are online and your last seen. To change it, 

- In the upper-right corner, click your personal avatar.
- Select **Account Settings**. 
- In the **Profile** section, either enable or disable the settings.

### **Logging out**

1. Click on your personal avatar at the top right of the workspace.
2. Click on Log out

image placeholder

### **Delete Account**

This action permanently deletes your account and all associated data.

**Caution**: This action cannot be undone. All your data, including workspaces, messages, channels and other data, will be permanently deleted.

To delete your account,

1. In the upper-right corner, click your personal avatar.
2. Select **Account Settings**. 
3. In the **Profile** section, click on **Delete Workspace.**

image placeholder

### **Notification Settings**

You can control the notifications you receive from these settings. To access it:

1. In the upper-right corner, click your personal avatar.
2. Select **Account Settings**. 
3. In the **Notifications **section, enable or disable required settings.

image placholder

You can also control the following notification settings related to:

- Mentions
- Task Assignments
- Thread Replies
- Direct Messages
- Weekly Digest

**Note**: These settings control email notifications only. You can still receive in-app notifications regardless of these settings. Unsubscribe links are included in all emails.
