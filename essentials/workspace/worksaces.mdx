\*\*What is a Workspace? \*\*

A Workspace is the primary container for your team's collaboration in Proddy. It houses all your channels, message history, and member directory. It serves as your comprehensive entry point; navigate from here to access settings, switch teams, or launch integrated features.

<img
  src="Images/Workspace/image8.png"

  style={{ width:"94%" }}
/>

### **Creating a Workspace**

To create a new workspace:

1. Open the sidebar
2. Click on the current workspace
3. Click Switch Workspace

   <img
     src="Images/Workspace/image6.png"
     alt="Profile section in Account Settings"
     style={{ width:"46%" }}
   />
4. Select Create Workspace
5. Enter new workspace name
6. Click Create

## **Joining a Workspace**

You can join an already existing workspace. For this, ask your team member to share a link and a code for the workspace. To learn more about sharing a workspace, <u>click here</u>.

Then,

1. Sign in to Proddy AI
2. Paste the link in your web browser.
3. Enter the 6-digit alphanumeric code shared with you.
4. You should now be able to access the shared workspace.

### **Renaming a Workspace**

To rename a template

1. Open the sidebar
2. Open Manage [https://www.proddy.tech/workspace/workspaceId/manage]
3. Under Workspaces find the Workspaces Settings
4. Type in your new workspace name in Workspace Settings section
5. Click Save

\
**Deleting a Workspace**

1. Open the sidebar
2. Open Manage [https://www.proddy.tech/workspace/workspaceId/manage]
3. Under Workspaces find the Workspaces Settings

<img
  src="Images/Workspace/image4.png"
  alt="Profile section in Account Settings"
  style={{ width:"91%" }}
/>

4. Click on Delete Workspace
5. Confirm your decision and click Delete

<img
  src="Images/Workspace/image11.png"
  alt="Profile section in Account Settings"
  style={{ width:"70%" }}
/>

**Caution**: This move is not reversible; please discuss it with your team before deleting.

**Switching between Workspaces**

If you are a member of multiple workspaces, you can switch between workspaces, reducing the hassle time to switch between apps. For this

1. Open the sidebar
2. Click on the current workspace
3. Click Switch Workspace
4. Either switch to an already existing workspace or create a new workspace

### **Working with a Workspace**

Your workspace is where you collaborate with your teammates to enhance your workflow. Think of it as your digital office. The dashboard of your workspace gives you a glimpse of everything that has happened and gives you an overview when you log in. This is where the magic starts.

**Proddy AI**

Proddy AI is your personal assistant to help you through your workflow. You can interact with it and ask for any doubts regarding the workspace.

To access it:

1. Open the sidebar.
2. Click on Proddy AI.

  src="Images/Workspace/image14.png"
  alt="Profile section in Account Settings"
  style={{ width:"100%" }}
/>

Go ahead and type in “What are some tasks assigned to me?” to start off a conversation.

- Notes
- Canvas

### **Notifications**

You can find all your notifications from your workspaces gathered in one place. These might be from your general channel, DMs or any tasks you have been assigned.

<img
  src="Images/Workspace/image1.png"
  alt="Profile section in Account Settings"

/>

To find your notifications, click on the notifications icon  on the top right of the workspace in the navigation bar.

**Search Bar**

The search bar provides an easy and effective way to search for anything inside your workspace.

To open the search bar either press

Control + K on Windows or

Command + K on Mac

Or click on the search bar on top of the Dashboard.

## **Customising Dashboard**

**Layout**

You can customise your dashboard to look and feel like you want. To move around and customise the dashboard, follow the steps as given below:

To resize views: 

1. Click the Edit option on the top right of the Workspace Overview pane.

   <img
     src="Images/Workspace/image10.png"
     alt="Profile section in Account Settings"
     style={{ width:"80%" }}
   />
1. Select the views you would like by adding or removing them.
2. Choose their sizes from the option given.

   <img
     src="Images/Workspace/image3.png"
     alt="Profile section in Account Settings"
     style={{ width:"80%" }}
   />

To change layout:

1. Find the  icon in the top right of the card in the edit mode.

   <img
     src="Images/Workspace/image2.png"
     alt="Profile section in Account Settings"
     style={{ width:"5%" }}
   />
3. Hold and drag it to move the card wherever you like it
