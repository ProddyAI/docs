## **Feedback**

To send feedback or request new features, click on the map icon  on the top right of the workspace in the navigation bar.

### **Documentation**

To find documentation and help regarding Proddy AI, click on the help icon  on the top right of the workspace in the navigation bar.

### **Chat Support**

To avail Chat Support in Proddy AI, click on the chat support icon  on the top right corner of the workspace in the navigation bar

You can either select from the set of questions or chat with us in the chat section available at the bottom of the Support Popup Menu.

<img
  src="Images/FeedBack/image7.png"
  alt="Profile section in Account Settings"
  style={{ width:"51%" }}
/>

You can also open Chat Support by clicking on the messages icon  at the bottom right corner in the workspace.
