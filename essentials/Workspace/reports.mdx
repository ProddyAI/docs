\*\*NOTE: \*\*This option is available only for workspace owners and administrators. To learn more on granting administrator access, <u>click here</u>.

**How to access it?**

\*\*Reports \*\*is available in the sidebar of your workspace.

1. Expand the sidebar.
2. Click on \*\*Reports **to open the**Reports & Analytics \*\* page.

<img
  src="Images/Reports/image4.png"
  alt="Profile section in Account Settings"
  style={{ width:"85%" }}
/>

### **Report Tabs**

**Workspace Overview**

This tab gives a general overview of all the statistics. This includes data on the number of users, messages sent, tasks created, and active channels and also shows progress regarding each member's task completion.

It also gives a diagrammatic description of daily messaging activity inside the workspace.

<img
  src="Images/Reports/image10.png"
  alt="Profile section in Account Settings"
  style={{ width:"85%" }}
/>

### **User Activity**

To access the \*\*Users \*\*tab:

1. Open the **Reports & Analytics** page.

Select \*\*Channels \*\*from the given tab menu

<img
  src="Images/Reports/image3.png"
  alt="Profile section in Account Settings"
  style={{ width:"85%" }}
/>

This tab gives in-depth details on each user's statistics. This includes the time spent on the workspace by each user.

**Channel Activity**

This tab shows statistics related to channel activity, including total messages sent. It categorises messages by channel, highlights the most active channel and provides much more insight into team participation in channels.

To access it

1. Open **Reports & Analytics** Page
2. Select \*\*Channels \*\*from the given tab menu.

### **Changing Time Range**

You can adjust the time range to see your statistics during your preferred time. To adjust it, first open the **Reports** tab. Then select from either 1 day, 7 days, or 30 days to see statistics for that time period.

By default it shows data for a 7-day period.

  src="Images/Reports/image12.png"

### **Export Report Details**

You can also download your **Reports & Analytics** to your computer. This can be done in two ways:

**Exporting as PDF**

To export as a PDF:

1. Open the \*\*Reports & Analytics \*\*page.
2. Find and click on the **Export PDF** option at the top right of the page

<img
  src="Images/Reports/image13.png"
  alt="Profile section in Account Settings"
  style={{ width:"40%" }}
/>

### **Exporting as JSON file**

To export as a JSON file:

1. Open the **Reports & Analytics **page.
2. Open the dropdown menu right next to the **Export PDF** option.
3. Select JSON from the dropdown menu.
